Traditional prediction markets suffer from several critical issues:

#### 1. **Lack of Trust**

- Centralized platforms control outcomes
- No transparency in resolution process
- Users must trust the platform operator
- Risk of manipulation and bias

#### 2. **High Barriers to Entry**

- Complex onboarding processes
- High minimum stakes required
- Geographic restrictions
- KYC/AML requirements

#### 3. **Limited Social Features**

- No way to create private markets with friends
- Can't build prediction communities
- Missing social engagement features
- No group competition mechanics

#### 4. **Poor User Experience**

- Complicated interfaces
- High gas fees for every action
- Slow transaction times
- Limited mobile support

#### 5. **Lack of Flexibility**

- Predefined market categories only
- Can't create custom markets
- Rigid market parameters
- No community governance
