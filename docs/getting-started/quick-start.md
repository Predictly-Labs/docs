## Step 5: Place Your First Vote

Let's make your first prediction!

### Choose a Market

1. Click on a market that interests you
2. Read the **description** carefully
3. Check the **deadline**
4. Review current **YES/NO percentages**

### Make Your Prediction

1. Click **"Vote YES"** or **"Vote NO"**
2. Enter your stake amount (e.g., `1` MOVE)
3. Review the transaction details:
   - Your prediction: YES or NO
   - Stake amount: X MOVE
   - Potential reward: ~Y MOVE (estimate)
4. Click **"Confirm"**
5. Approve the transaction in your wallet
6. âœ… Vote placed!

**Example:**

```
Market: "Will Bitcoin reach $100k by end of 2025?"
Your Vote: YES
Stake: 1 MOVE
Current Pool: 10 MOVE YES, 5 MOVE NO
Your Potential Reward: ~1.5 MOVE (if YES wins)
```

## Common Questions

### How much does it cost?

- **Creating markets**: FREE (backend pays gas)
- **Voting**: You pay gas (~0.0005 MOVE)
- **Claiming**: You pay gas (~0.0005 MOVE)

### Can I change my vote?

No, votes are final once submitted. Choose carefully!

### What if I lose?

You lose your stake. It goes to the winners' reward pool.

### What is "Zero Loss" market?

A special market type where your principal is protected through DeFi yield farming. Coming soon!

### How are markets resolved?

Trusted judges (assigned by group admins) resolve markets based on real-world outcomes.
