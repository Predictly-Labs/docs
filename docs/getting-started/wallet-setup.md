## Installation Guide

### Nightly Wallet Setup

#### Desktop (Chrome Extension)

1. **Visit** [wallet.nightly.app](https://wallet.nightly.app/)
2. Click **"Download"**
3. Select **"Chrome Extension"**
4. Click **"Add to Chrome"**
5. Click **"Add Extension"**
6. Pin the extension to your toolbar

#### Mobile App

1. **iOS**: Download from [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/nightly-wallet)
2. **Android**: Download from [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=app.nightly.wallet)

#### Create Wallet

1. Open Nightly Wallet
2. Click **"Create New Wallet"**

   - ‚ö†Ô∏è **CRITICAL**: Store this safely offline

6. ‚úÖ Wallet created!

## Connecting to Predictly

### First-Time Connection

1. **Open Predictly** application
2. Click **"Connect Wallet"** button (top right)
3. **Select** your wallet from the list:
   - Nightly
   - Petra
   - Martian
4. **Approve** connection request in wallet popup
5. ‚úÖ Connected!

### What Predictly Can Access

When you connect, Predictly can:

- ‚úÖ View your wallet address
- ‚úÖ Request transaction signatures
- ‚úÖ Check your MOVE balance

Predictly **CANNOT**:

- ‚ùå Access your private keys
- ‚ùå Move funds without your approval
- ‚ùå Make transactions without your signature

### Disconnecting

To disconnect your wallet:

**In Predictly:**

1. Click your wallet address (top right)
2. Click **"Disconnect"**

**In Wallet:**

1. Open wallet extension
2. Go to **"Connected Sites"**
3. Find Predictly
4. Click **"Disconnect"**

## Wallet Security

### Best Practices

#### ‚úÖ DO:

- **Backup recovery phrase** on paper
- **Store backup** in safe place (fireproof safe, safety deposit box)
- **Use strong password**
- **Enable 2FA** if available
- **Verify URLs** before connecting
- **Keep software updated**
- **Use hardware wallet** for large amounts

#### ‚ùå DON'T:

- **Share recovery phrase** with anyone
- **Screenshot recovery phrase**
- **Store digitally** (cloud, email, notes app)
- **Use same password** as other accounts
- **Connect to unknown sites**
- **Click suspicious links**
- **Share private keys**

### Recovery Phrase

Your recovery phrase is the **ONLY** way to recover your wallet if:

- You forget your password
- You lose your device
- Extension gets corrupted
- You want to access from another device

**‚ö†Ô∏è WARNING:**

- Anyone with your recovery phrase can access your funds
- There's no customer support to recover lost phrases
- Predictly team will NEVER ask for your recovery phrase

### Recognizing Scams

**Common Scams:**

üö´ **Fake Support**

- "Support" asking for recovery phrase
- DMs offering to "help" with wallet issues

üö´ **Phishing Sites**

- Fake Predictly websites
- URLs with slight misspellings

üö´ **Airdrop Scams**

- "Free tokens" requiring wallet connection
- Suspicious transaction approvals

**Stay Safe:**

- ‚úÖ Bookmark official Predictly URL
- ‚úÖ Verify URLs before connecting
- ‚úÖ Never share recovery phrase
- ‚úÖ Be skeptical of "too good to be true" offers

## Troubleshooting

### Wallet Won't Connect

**Solutions:**

1. Refresh Predictly page
2. Disconnect and reconnect wallet
3. Clear browser cache
4. Update wallet extension
5. Try different browser

### Transaction Stuck

**Solutions:**

1. Wait 1-2 minutes
2. Check network status
3. Increase gas fee (if option available)
4. Contact support if still stuck

### Can't See Balance

**Solutions:**

1. Refresh wallet
2. Check network (Testnet vs Mainnet)
3. Verify you're on Movement Network
4. Wait for network sync

### Lost Recovery Phrase

**Unfortunately:**

- ‚ùå Cannot be recovered
- ‚ùå No customer support can help
- ‚ùå Funds may be lost

**Prevention:**

- ‚úÖ Write down immediately
- ‚úÖ Store multiple copies
- ‚úÖ Use metal backup plates for fire protection

You can use multiple wallets with Predictly:
